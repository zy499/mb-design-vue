## zh-CN

通过 `checked-color` 和 `unchecked-color` 可以自定义开关的颜色。

---

## en-US

The color of the switch can be customized through `checked-color` and `unchecked-color`.

---

```vue
<template>
  <a-switch checked-color="#F53F3F" unchecked-color="#14C9C9" />
</template>
```
